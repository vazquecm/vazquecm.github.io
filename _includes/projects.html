Selected Projects

  • Listen To Me Please!

    This app was designed to help facilitate the communication between myself and specifically my nephew who has Autism & Down Syndrome. He does not have strong verbal skills but is able to use an iPad. So with this app, he can look for an image and decide if it is something that he wants to convey to me. When he then clicks on the image an email is generated and sent to me. This allows him to feel and become more independent all the while allowing those who care for him know what he actually needs.
